About Me

Hey there! I’m Jay, a self-taught photographer, editor, and visual storyteller based in Gingoog City, Misamis Oriental. I wasn’t born with a camera in my hand, but I was born with curiosity, creativity, and the kind of hunger that no one could ignore.

I started my journey in the simplest way possible, with just a smartphone and a dream. Back when I was in my first year of college, I didn’t have a fancy camera, editing software, or any training. What I had was passion. I started editing my photos using low-budget mobile apps, experimenting with filters and enhancements I discovered through TikTok tutorials, YouTube videos, and free sites. Every shot I took was a lesson. Every mistake taught me something new.

I still remember how I'd stay up late watching tutorials, replaying the same steps until I got them right, sometimes with shaky hands, blurry shots, and busted storage space. But I didn’t stop. From first year to fourth year, I kept grinding. I borrowed equipment when I could, practiced on friends, shot anything I could, birthdays, walks, sunsets, shadows on walls, just to keep improving.

Eventually, my parents saw how serious I was. They saw the fire I had. And one day, they surprised me with a Nikon D3200. I thanks god for this unforgetable gift that moment changed my life. It wasn't just a gift, it was the key that unlocked a whole new world. Holding that camera for the first time felt like holding my future. It wasn’t perfect or brand-new, but it was mine. And with it, I built something real.

From there, I dove deeper, learning composition, lighting, storytelling, and color grading. I studied the way emotions moved through people’s faces, how light kissed the skin, how stories could live forever in a single frame. My camera became more than just gear. It became my voice.

Today, I specialize in capturing raw, real, and beautiful moments, whether it’s a quiet solo portrait, a chaotic barkada shoot, a love-filled prenup, or a powerful cinematic short film. Every shoot I do is a collaboration of souls, stories, and light.

I know what it’s like to start from nothing. I know how powerful it is when someone believes in you. So if you’re reading this, maybe you’re a student, a dreamer, or just someone who wants to be seen through a lens that understands, know this: you’re already enough. And if I’m available, I can make you a photo shoot that brings out the best in you. Just message me. Let’s create something real.
